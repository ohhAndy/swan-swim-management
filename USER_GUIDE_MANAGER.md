# Swan Swim Management - Manager Training Guide

**Target Audience:** Managers & Administrators
**Scope:** Student Management & Daily Operations.

---

## 1. Student & Family Management

### Creating New Students
1.  Navigate to **Students** > **New Student**.
2.  **Guardian Info**: Enter full parent/guardian details including phone and email.
3.  **Student Info**: Enter name, birthdate, and medical notes.
4.  **Level**: Select the appropriate swim level (e.g., "Parent & Tot", "Preschool 1").

### Profile Views
Understanding the layout of Student and Guardian profiles is key to quick management.

#### Student Profile
- **Information Card**: Displays Level, Birthdate, and Age. Click "Edit" to update levels or personal info.
- **Guardian Panel**: Quick link to the parent's full profile and contact details.
- **Enrollment History**: Shows all Active (Green) and Past enrollments.
    - **Invoices**: Badges show payment status (Paid, Partial, Not Invoiced).
    - **Actions**: Use the "Transfer" button here to move a student to a new class.

#### Guardian Profile
- **Contact Details**: Email, Phone, and Waiver Status (Signed/Pending).
- **Notes**: A dedicated section (yellow box) for family-specific notes (e.g., "Grandmother picks up on Tuesdays").
- **Linked Students**: A list of all children under this guardian, showing their active classes and levels.

### Managing Enrollments
- **New Enrollment**: From a Student Profile, click "Add Enrollment" and select a class.
- **Transfers**: To move a student, open their current enrollment and click "Transfer".

### Scheduling Makeups & Trials
Both are scheduled directly from the **Weekly Schedule View**.
1.  **Navigate to the Weekly Schedule**:
    - Click **Schedule** in the main sidebar.
    - You will see cards for each day of the week (Monday - Sunday).
    - Click on the specific **Time Slot button** (e.g., "4:00 PM - 4:30 PM") inside the desired Day card.
2.  **Locate the Class**: Scroll to find the specific class offering column.
3.  **Add Booking**: At the bottom of the column (below the student list), you will see "Add" buttons (visible on hover).
    - **Makeup**: Click `+ Makeup` to book a makeup for an existing student.
    - **Trial**: Click `+ Trial` to book a trial for a prospective student.

## 2. Class Scheduling & Management

### Creating a New Class
1.  **Navigate to the Time Slot**:
    - Go to **Schedule** > Select Day Card > Click Specific **Time Slot**.
2.  **Add Class**: In the top header of the view, click the **"Add Class"** button.
3.  **Define**: Type in the appropriate offering title and confirm. The new class column will appear instantly.

### Managing Classes
1.  **Edit Offering**: Click the "Edit" button next to the class title to change the offering name/level.
2.  **Delete Class**: Click the **Trash Icon** in the class header.
    - *Note: You cannot delete a class if it has active enrollments.*

## 3. Daily Operations & Attendance

### Taking Attendance
1.  Navigate to the **Schedule** tab > **Today**.
2.  Open the **Daily Roster** for the specific class.
3.  Click the status button next to the student's name:
    - **Present**: Student is in the water.
    - **Absent**: Student is not present.
    - **Excused**: Student called ahead.

### Report Cards
1.  On the Daily Roster, click the **Card** status badge.
2.  Update to:
    - **Created**: When the instructor completes the card.
    - **Given**: When the card is handed to the family.

### Instructor Assignment
1.  On the Regular Schedule or Daily Schedule (Date View), click the **Edit (Pencil)** icon next to the instructor name.
2.  Select the **Instructors**.

## 4. Dashboard & Analytics

### Key Metrics
The dashboard provides a real-time snapshot of the swim school's health:
- **Students per Day**: Shows the distribution of student volume across the week.
- **Enrollments per Term**: Tracks total active enrollments for the current and upcoming terms.

### Action Items
- **Pending Tasks**: Monitors operational items that need attention, such as:
    - Unpaid drop-in fees.
    - Missing waivers.
    - Student alerts.
