# 📋 Report Card Quick‑Start Guide (Supervisors)

## 1️⃣ Open a Report Card
1. In **Daily Schedule**, locate the student row you want to grade.
2. Click the **card icon** (or “Report Card” button) on that row.
3. A dialog titled **“Report Card: {Student Name}”** appears.

---

## 2️⃣ Choose the Level
- The **Level** dropdown is always available, even after the card is locked.
- Select the student’s current level (pre‑selected) or switch to an earlier level **only to view** its skill list.
- **You cannot edit** past levels once the card is submitted.

---

## 3️⃣ Rate Each Skill

| Rating | Meaning (plain language) |
|--------|---------------------------|
| ⚪ **Not Started** | The student hasn’t begun this skill. |
| 🟡 **Developing** | The student is working on it but hasn’t mastered it yet. |
| 🟢 **Mastered** | The student can perform the skill confidently. |

- Click the button next to each skill to set its rating.
- The selected rating gets a coloured background (visual confirmation).

> **Tip:** Rate *all* listed skills before moving on. The system will allow a save, but will warn you if any skill is left blank.

---

## 4️⃣ Add Comments (optional)
- Below the skill list is a **Comments** box.
- Write any extra notes for the student or guardian (e.g., “Needs extra practice on breathing”).
- Leaving it empty is fine.

---

## 5️⃣ Preview the Report Card (recommended)
1. Click the **eye icon** (“Preview”) at the top‑right.
2. A PDF‑style preview appears.
3. Review layout, wording, and comments.
4. Close the preview to return to the form.

---

## 6️⃣ Save / Submit

| Button | What it does |
|--------|--------------|
| **Save Report Card** | Saves the current state as a **draft**. You can come back later to finish it. |
| **Submit & Complete** (appears when status = “Completed”) | Locks the card, marks it **completed**, and **automatically upgrades** the student to the next level. **After this you cannot edit anything.** |
| **Cancel** (the “X” in the corner) | Closes the dialog **without** saving changes made in this session. |

**Workflow**
- Click **Save** while you’re still working.
- When the card is final, click **Submit & Complete**.
- A confirmation dialog asks: *“Are you sure you want to mark this report card as Completed?”* – confirm to lock the card.

---

## 7️⃣ Email Guardians (one‑time only)
- After the card is saved (draft or completed), open the **Preview** dialog.
- Click **“Email Guardians”** (mail icon).
- The system sends a PDF copy to the student’s guardians.
- **The button disables after a successful send** – you can’t email the same report twice.

---

## ✅ Do’s
- **Do** fill in every skill rating before submitting.
- **Do** use **Preview** to double‑check the layout and comments.
- **Do** click **Submit & Complete** only when you’re certain the report is final.
---

## ❌ Don’ts
- **Don’t** edit a report after clicking **Submit & Complete** – it becomes read‑only.
- **Don’t** submit and complete unless you are sure this is the final report card you want to save and send to parents. 

---

## 📚 Quick FAQ

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| **Can I edit a submitted report card?** | No. After **Submit & Complete**, the card is read‑only. You can only view other levels. |
| **What happens when I submit?** | The student’s level automatically advances to the next one and the report card is locked. |
| **What if I send the email by accident?** | Email can be sent **once** per report card.
| **I made a mistake before submitting – can I fix it?** | Yes, as long as the card is still in **Draft** status. Click **Save** after correcting it. |
