# Swan Swim Management - Application Feature Overview

## 1. Executive Dashboard
The central hub for business intelligence and daily operations.
- **Key Metrics**: Real-time view of "Students per Day" and "Enrollments per Term".
- **Daily Operations**: Quick access to today's schedule and student counters.
- **Action Items**: "Pending Tasks" widget for staff coordination.

## 2. Smart Scheduling System
A high-performance scheduling engine designed for complex swim school operations.
- **Weekday Schedule View**: A parallel-optimized weekly view that allows instant visibility into class load across all 7 days.
- **Student Grid (Timeslot View)**:
    - **Rapid Navigation**: Optimized "Next/Previous" flow for seamless browsing.
    - **Visual Capacity**: Clear indicators for class capacity, instructor assignment, and student ratios (e.g., 3:1, 1:1, 2:1).
    - **Level Grouping**: distinct color-coding for "Parent & Tot", "Preschool", "Swimmer", and "Swim Team" levels.

## 3. Student & Enrollment Management
Comprehensive CRM capabilities for student lifecycles.
- **Profiles**: Detailed student records including medical notes, birthdates, and guardians.
- **Flexible Enrollment**:
    - **Standard Enrollment**: Term-based automated booking.
    - **Trial Classes**: One-off booking flow for potential new students.
    - **Make-ups**: Integrated system for scheduling make-up classes with conflict detection.
    - **Transfers**: Seamless logic to move enrollments between slots/days.

## 4. Financial Management
Integrated financial tracking tightly coupled with enrollments.
- **Invoicing**: Automated invoice generation linked to student enrollments.
- **Payment Tracking**: History of payments, partial payments, and outstanding balances.
- **Reporting**: Exportable financial reports for reconciliation.

## 5. Staff & Administration
Tools for managing the team and configuring the system.
- **Task Management**: Built-in task tracker for staff to assign and complete daily duties.
- **Role-Based Access**: Granular permissions for Admins, Managers, and Instructors.
- **Term Configuration**: Tools to create new terms, define holiday closures, and set global schedule templates.
