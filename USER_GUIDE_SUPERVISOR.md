# Swan Swim Management - Supervisor Training Guide

**Target Audience:** Deck Supervisors
**Scope:** Daily Floor Management & Attendance Only.

> **Note:** Supervisors do not have access to sensitive student or guardian information, billing, or enrollment changes.

---

## 1. Dashboard & Tasks
Your central hub for daily operations.

### Pending Tasks
- Check the **"Pending Tasks"** widget on the dashboard daily.
- Resolve simple operational items assigned to you.

---

## 2. Daily Schedule Management

### Viewing the Schedule
1.  Navigate to **Schedule**.
2.  Your view is restricted to the **Current Date**.
3.  You will see the **Daily Roster** for all classes running today.

### Assigning Instructors
If a class needs an instructor assigned (or changed):
1.  Locate the class on the Daily Schedule.
2.  Click the **Edit (Pencil)** icon next to the instructor's name.
3.  Select the correct **Instructor** from the dropdown list.
4.  Save the assignment.

---

## 3. Class Operations

### Taking Attendance
Attendance must be accurate for safety and makeup eligibility.
1.  On the **Daily Roster**, find the student.
2.  Click the status button.
3.  Select:
    - **Present**: Student is in the water.
    - **Absent**: Student is not present (no notification).
    - **Excused**: Student called ahead.

### Student Remarks
Use remarks to note daily progress or behavioral issues.
1.  Click the **Remarks** icon (or "Edit/View Remarks" button) next to the student.
2.  Enter the note (e.g., "Passed Level 3 requirements").
3.  Save.

### Report Cards
You are responsible for tracking the status of student report cards.
1.  On the **Daily Roster**, locate the "Card" column (or badge).
2.  Click the status badge to update it:
    - **Not Created**: Default state.
    - **Created**: The instructor has filled out the card.
    - **Given**: The card has been handed to the parent/student.
